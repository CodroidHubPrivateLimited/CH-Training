How to Make Margherita Pizza

Description

This classic Margherita pizza features a crisp crust, tangy tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves.

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Yield: 1 large pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 10-12 fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with a pizza stone if you have one.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Arrange the mozzarella slices evenly over the sauce.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and oregano if using.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve immediately.

Tips

  • Use a pizza stone preheated for at least 30 minutes for the crispiest crust.
  • For best results, use fresh mozzarella di bufala if available.
  • Let the dough come to room temperature and rest for 30 minutes before shaping.
  • Add a final drizzle of high-quality olive oil after baking for extra flavor.
  • For a more authentic flavor, use San Marzano tomatoes for the sauce.
