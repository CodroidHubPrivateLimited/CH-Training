Manali - Valley of the Gods

Gateway to the Himalayas

Manali, nestled in the Kullu Valley Elevation: 2,050 meters (6,726 ft) , is a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh known for its scenic beauty and adventure activities.

Key Attractions:

  • Hadimba Temple - Ancient cave temple
  • Solang Valley - Adventure sports hub
  • Old Manali - Hippie culture and cafes
  • Vashisht Hot Springs - Natural sulfur springs

Travel Information

Best Time to Visit March-June (summer), October-January (winter)
Nearest Airport Bhuntar (Kullu-Manali Airport, 50km)
Main Bus Station Manali Bus Stand (HRTC)
Famous For Honeymoon destination, trekking base, apple orchards

Important Notes:

Manali serves as the base camp for:

  1. Rohtang Pass excursions
  2. Journeys through Atal Tunnel to Lahaul-Spiti
  3. Trekking routes to Hampta Pass and Beas Kund
