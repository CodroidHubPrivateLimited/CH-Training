Atal Tunnel & Rohtang La

Experience the engineering marvel of Atal Tunnel World's longest highway tunnel above 10,000 feet and the breathtaking Rohtang Pass Ancient trade route at 13,058 ft elevation in the Himalayas.

Key Highlights:

  • 9.02 km long Atal Tunnel (formerly Rohtang Tunnel)
  • Reduces travel time from Manali to Leh by 4-5 hours
  • All-weather connectivity
  • Spectacular mountain views

Best time to visit: May to October. Altitude warning May cause altitude sickness - acclimatize properly
