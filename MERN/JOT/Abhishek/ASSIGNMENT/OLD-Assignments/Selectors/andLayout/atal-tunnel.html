Atal Tunnel

Engineering Marvel

The Atal Tunnel, named after former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee, is a high-altitude tunnel Located at 10,040 feet in the Pir Panjal range that connects Manali to Lahaul-Spiti valley.

Technical Specifications:

  • Length: 9.02 km
  • Width: 10 meters
  • Height: 5.52 meters
  • Completed: October 2020

Travel Information

Best Time to Visit Year-round (first all-weather tunnel in Himalayas)
Entry Point South Portal (Manali side)
Travel Time Saved 4-5 hours compared to Rohtang Pass route
