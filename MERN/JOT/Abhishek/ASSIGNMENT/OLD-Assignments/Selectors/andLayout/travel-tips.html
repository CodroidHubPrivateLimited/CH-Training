Essential Himalayan Travel Tips

Altitude Sickness Prevention

When traveling to high-altitude areas Above 8,000ft/2,400m requires acclimatization like Rohtang Pass:

  • Ascend gradually (max 1,000ft/300m per day above 8,000ft)
  • Stay hydrated (3-4 liters water daily)
  • Avoid alcohol and smoking
  • Consider Diamox after consulting doctor

Packing Checklist

Category Essential Items
Clothing Layered woolens, waterproof jacket, gloves, hat
Footwear Sturdy trekking shoes with good grip
Health Medications, sunscreen (SPF 50+), lip balm, sunglasses
Documents ID proofs, permits (for restricted areas), travel insurance

Road Travel Advice

  • Check weather conditions Landslides common during monsoon (July-Aug) before departure
  • Fuel up in Manali - no stations beyond
  • Carry tire chains in winter (Nov-Mar)
  • Hire experienced local drivers for mountain roads
