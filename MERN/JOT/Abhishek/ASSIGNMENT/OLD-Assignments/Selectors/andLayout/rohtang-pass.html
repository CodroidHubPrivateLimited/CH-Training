Rohtang Pass

Historical Mountain Pass

Rohtang Pass (13,058 ft 3,980 meters above sea level ) is a high mountain pass on the eastern Pir Panjal Range of the Himalayas.

Key Features:

  • Connects Kullu Valley with Lahaul and Spiti Valleys
  • Name means "pile of corpses" due to difficult crossings
  • Open only May-November due to heavy snowfall
  • Stunning views of glaciers and peaks

Visitor Information

Best Time to Visit June to September
Permit Required Yes (can be obtained online or in Manali)
Distance from Manali 51 km (about 4-5 hour drive)

Note: After opening of Atal Tunnel, Rohtang Pass is mainly for tourism purposes.
